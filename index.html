About Our Studio

Who We Are

Mblue Game is an independent mobile gaming studio owned and operated by Onur Uslu. Founded with a vision to build highly engaging, polished, and creative digital experiences, we blend advanced development methodologies with community-driven designs.

Our Mission & Core Values

Our mission is simple: to engineer memorable mobile games that give players an escape from daily stress while challenging their skills. We base our workflow on three core developer commitments:

  • Uncompromising Quality: Ensuring bug-free, smooth performance across various mid-range and high-end Android devices.
  • Artistic Originality: Creating striking minimalist visual assets and seamless control mechanics tailored for mobile touch screens.
  • Player-First Support: Maintaining open communication channels for direct performance reports and community optimization ideas.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mblue Game ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Onur Uslu, handles information collected through our mobile applications (including MBStress: Boss Rush) and this official website.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our applications are built as commercial products. We do not explicitly collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as your legal name, physical address, or phone number directly. However, our games integrated with third-party store services (like Amazon Appstore or Palm Store) might process automated device data for analytics and operational health.

2. Third-Party Services & Stores

Our products utilize verified third-party distribution SDKs which may collect standard data used to identify devices. Please review the external privacy guidelines of our distribution networks:

  • Amazon Appstore Privacy Notice
  • Palm Store / Transsion Service Regulations

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security safeguards to protect any localized device configurations or internal high scores. No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, but we continuously update our software baselines to minimize security risks.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games comply with international standards regarding children's online data safety. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided external log data, please reach out via our official support email immediately so we can execute necessary removal workflows.

5. Contact Information

For official legal data inquiries, policy interpretations, or explicit data requests regarding our applications, please contact us directly via our formal data compliance channels.
